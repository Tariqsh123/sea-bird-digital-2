Frequently Asked Questions

What is AI ghostwriting?

+

AI ghostwriting uses artificial intelligence to write content on your behalf, capturing your voice and style so the final product appears to be authored by you.

How does the AI capture my voice?

+

We analyse samples of your existing writing, interview you about your style, and use custom training to align tone, vocabulary, and phrasing with your personality.

What types of content can AI ghostwriting produce?

+

AI ghostwriting can create books, ebooks, blog posts, articles, whitepapers, case studies, social media content, newsletters, and more — all in your authentic voice.

How long does it take to complete a project?

+

Turnaround depends on length and complexity. A typical blog post can be drafted in hours, while a full manuscript may take a few weeks, with iterative collaboration throughout.

Who owns the rights to the final content?

+

You own 100% of the final content. As a ghostwriting service, we transfer all rights to you, so you can publish, edit, or reuse it as you see fit.
