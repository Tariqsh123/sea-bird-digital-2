HOW WE WORK

Our AI Character Design Process

A collaborative process that turns your brief into a cast of compelling, production‑ready characters.

01

Discovery

We discuss your project, narrative, art style, and technical needs. We review references and establish creative direction.

02

Generate

Our AI produces initial character concepts, variations, and mood boards based on your brief and references.

03

Refine

Distinctive Visual Identity

+
  • Unique silhouette and proportions.
  • Memorable color palette and costume.

Consistency & Versatility

+

Frequently Asked Questions

What is AI character design?

+

AI character design uses generative AI to create original characters for games, animation, and virtual experiences — from concept sketches to fully rendered turnaround sheets.

Can I request specific styles or references?

+

Absolutely. We work from your mood boards, art references, and style preferences to ensure every character aligns with your creative vision.

What file formats do you deliver?

+

We provide high‑resolution PNG, PSD, vector files, and optional 3D‑ready turnarounds, depending on your production needs.

How many iterations can I expect?

+

Our process includes multiple rounds of generation and refinement, giving you plenty of options and the ability to fine‑tune until the character is perfect.

Do you offer full character sheets and expressions?

+

Yes. We provide complete character sheets including front, back, side views, and a range of facial expressions and gesture poses for animation reference.
